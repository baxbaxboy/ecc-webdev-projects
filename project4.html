SHIPPING INFORMATION

Domestic US Shipping: We use a mixture of USPS First Class Mail, USPS Priority Mail, and UPS Ground for domestic orders. Shipping prices are calculated by weight, zip code, and dimensions. The US rates below are maximum shipping charges. Most orders will have a lower price option. Customers in Alaska and Hawaii please allow 5-7 business days for USPS Priority Mail delivery. Shipping is calculated during the checkout process once you enter the state and zip code of the shipping address.

International Shipping: We use USPS First Class mail for all international orders weighing 4 pounds or less. We use USPS Global Priority Mail for all orders weighing more than 4 pounds. Refer to the tables below for international shipping prices. Please note: On January 17, 2016 the United States Postal Service increased all international First Class and Priority Mail rates. The rate increases are significant. Our shipping prices are exactly what USPS charges us for shipping. We do not profit off shipping prices.

How to Save Money on International Shipping: As you can see in the tables above, USPS charges the same postage rate for 9 ounces to 2 pounds. To reduce your "per piece" shipping cost, order as close to 2 pounds as possible. Example: To ship 2 large shirts to Europe costs $23.49 for shipping or $11.75 per shirt. An order for 4 large shirts going to Europe costs the same $23.49 to ship, but the per piece shipping cost reduces to $5.88 per shirt. Garment weights are listed on the Garment Sizing page. There is a link to the page in each product description or you can click here.

Undeliverable and Unclaimed Orders: If your order is returned to us as unclaimed or undeliverable, we will notify you and offer to ship it to you again, but you will need to pay 50% of the original shipping charge. If you refuse the 50% shipping charge or do not reply to the notification we will issue a credit for the merchandise portion of your order. We will not refund the original shipping charge.
